Welcome to Huntsville Lodge No. 364


We represent a fraternity which believes in justice and truth and honorable action in your community... men who are endeavoring to be better citizen...[and] to make a great country greater. This is the only institution in the world where we can meet on the level all sorts of people who want to live rightly.
Bro. Harry S. Truman

Huntsville Lodge No. 364 is a Lodge of Free and Accepted Masons located at Huntsville Arkansas

Our Lodge's physical address is:

300 Main Street, Huntsville Arkansas

(across the street from the Madison County Court House.)



Our stated Communications are held on the 2nd and 4th Tuesdays each month,
Dinner is served beginning at 6:00 P.M. Lodge opens at 7:00 P.M.



Non-Masons are always welcome to visit us and have dinner with the brethren between 6:00 P.M. and 6:45 P.M.



Schedule of Events 2023


Tuesday 27 December (2022) Huntsville Lodge No. 364 and Little Springs Lodge No. 230 held a joint installation of officers during the Festival of Saint John the Evangelist. A banquet was held prior to the festivities in the Lodge dining room with Worshipful Brother Rodney Fry, Junior Past Master presiding. Following dinner, the officers of both lodges were installed into their respective offices with Most Worshipful Kevin L. Hatfield, PGM serving as installing officer and Worshipful Brother Michael Foster, P.M. as installing Marshal.


Tuesday 10 January Stated Communication of Huntsville Lodge No. 364; Dinner served at 6:00 p.m.; Lodge opens at 7:00 p.m.


Monday 16 January Called Communication of Huntsville Lodge No. 364; Dinner served at 5:30 p.m.; Lodge opens at 6:00 p.m. Master's Degree conferral on Bro. Dillan Welch, (a 4th generation Mason).


Tuesday 17 January Stated Communication of Little Springs Lodge No. 230; Dinner served at 6:00 p.m.; Lodge opens at 7:00 p.m. The Lodge will confer an EA Degree on Mr. J. W. Bohannon as a courtesy for Huntsville Lodge.


Tuesday 24 January Stated Communication of Huntsville Lodge No. 364; Dinner served at 6:00 p.m.; Lodge opens at 7:00 p.m. (Update: Communication cancelled due to inclement weather (Heavy snow!!)


Tuesday 31 January (5th Tuesday) Re-scheduled Communication of Huntsville Lodge No. 364 from last week; Dinner served at 6:00 p.m.; Lodge opens at 7:00 p.m.
